Foreign Influence Explorer Schema

The foreign influence database accessed via the Sunlight Foundation's Influence Explorer is updated with a data entry process using the Department of Justice' Foreign Agents Registration Act records. Downloads are updated daily. It should be noted, however, that not all documents submitted to the Department of Justice under the Foreign Agents Registration Act have been processed by us. Currently, users of our site can access all of the records submitted in 2013. A portion of the documents submitted in 2011 and 2012 have been processed by us and are available on our site. We are working hard to standardize and make available for browsing and download as many documents as we can. Information is available if you would like to learn how to contribute to this project.

Contributions

Note: Contribution amounts will likely NOT match records available at the Federal Election Commission, or those housed by the Sunlight Foundation's Influence Explorer and on OpenSecrets.org. Differences in reporting requirements between the FEC and the DOJ account for differences in amounts reported as well as different contribution dates.

date
The date of contact. Dates with an asterisk represent the date on the document.
amount
The dollar amount of the contribution.
recipient
The campaign, political action committee or individual receiving the contribution.
registrant
The person or organization that is filing, also known as the foreign agent.
contributing_individual_or_pac
The name of the political action committee or person making the contribution.
recipient_crp_id
This is the unique identifier issued to members of Congress by the Center for Responsive Politics and would be used to identify contributions to that member. In the case of leadership PACs, the member's ID is assigned to the PAC with the name of the PAC in the Recipient field.
recipient_bioguide_id
This is the unique identifier issued to members of Congress by the government and would be used to identify contributions to that member. In the case of leadership PACs, the member's ID is assigned to the PAC with the name of the PAC in the Recipient field.
